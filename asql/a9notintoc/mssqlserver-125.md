# MSSQLSERVER_125
  
## Details  
  
|||  
|-|-|  
|Product Name|SQL Server|  
|Event ID|125|  
|Event Source|MSSQLSERVER|  
|Component|SQLEngine|  
|Symbolic Name||  
|Message Text|Case expressions may only be nested to level %d.|  
  
## Explanation  
[!INCLUDE[ssNoVersion](../a9notintoc/includes/ssnoversion-md.md)] allows for only 10 levels of nesting in CASE expressions.  
  
## User Action  
Reduce the level of CASE statements to 10 or less.
